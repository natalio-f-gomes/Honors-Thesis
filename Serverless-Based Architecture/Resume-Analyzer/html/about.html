    About Resume-Analyzer

    A research project designed to help students improve their resumes through our analysis and personalized feedback

    Our Story

    Born from real student struggles

    From Frustration to Innovation

    ResumeScanner was born out of a genuine need experienced by its creator during the summer of 2023. After applying to hundreds of software engineering internships and facing countless rejections without feedback, the question arose: "What if there was a way to get constructive feedback on resumes before applying?"

    This project represents more than just a web application—it's a research initiative developed as part of the Adrian Tinsley Program (ATP) at Bridgewater State University, aimed at solving a real problem that affects countless students in their job search journey.

    Research Methodology

    This project follows a structured software development lifecycle as part of the Adrian Tinsley Program research initiative. Our methodology integrates academic research with practical application, ensuring both scientific rigor and real-world utility.

    Research Question

    How can technology be used to provide job seekers with constructive feedback on their resumes and provide resources where users can develop those skills?

    Target Outcome

    A functional web application that generates personalized feedback and resources to support users in improving their resumes and enhancing their job search success.
