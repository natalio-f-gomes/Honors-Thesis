Our Story

Born from real student struggles

From Frustration to Innovation

ResumeScanner was born out of a genuine need experienced by its creator during the summer of 2023. After applying to hundreds of software engineering internships and facing countless rejections without feedback, the question arose: "What if there was a way to get constructive feedback on resumes before applying?"

This project represents more than just a web applicationâ€”it's a research initiative developed as part of the Adrian Tinsley Program (ATP) at Bridgewater State University, aimed at solving a real problem that affects countless students in their job search journey.

How It Works

Advanced NLP technology meets practical career guidance

Intelligent Resume Analysis

Our Natural Language Processing algorithms analyze your resume's content, structure, keywords, and formatting to provide comprehensive, personalized feedback.

  • NLP-powered content analysis
  • Keyword optimization suggestions
  • ATS compatibility scoring
Job Description Matching

Compare your resume against real job descriptions from platforms like LinkedIn and Indeed to identify gaps and opportunities for improvement.

  • Real-time job market analysis
  • Skill gap identification
  • Industry-specific feedback
BSU Resources Integration

Get personalized recommendations for BSU's academic and career services, including tutoring, courses, and on-campus job opportunities.

  • Campus resource recommendations
  • Academic program guidance
  • Career center integration
Curated Learning Resources

Access a carefully curated list of online courses, certifications, and learning materials to help you develop the skills identified in your analysis.

  • Skill development recommendations
  • Certification guidance
  • Learning pathway suggestions

Research Methodology

This project follows a structured software development lifecycle as part of the Adrian Tinsley Program research initiative. Our methodology integrates academic research with practical application, ensuring both scientific rigor and real-world utility.

Research Question

How can technology be used to provide job seekers with constructive feedback on their resumes and provide resources where users can develop those skills?

Target Outcome

A functional web application that generates personalized feedback and resources to support users in improving their resumes and enhancing their job search success.
